# Thrain Ironfist's Clan Elders

## Group Type
- Dwarven leadership council (Ironfist Clan)

## Status
- Unmet (referenced)

## Why They Matter
- They may call Thrain back for political or military reasons.
- They hold knowledge of ancient dwarven tunnels and possibly a ward anchor.
- Their stance could sway the entire Ironfist Clan.

## Campaign Relevance
- Potential gatekeepers to dwarven reinforcements, logistics, and tunnel access.
- Could validate or challenge Thrain's authority in the field.

## Possible First Contact
- A formal summons delivered to Thrain.
- An elder envoy arriving at Stonebridge or on the road east.
