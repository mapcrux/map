# Vaelanthir, the Sundered One (Before His Fall)

## Group Type
- Historical figure (elven archmage)

## Status
- Historical

## Why They Matter
- Once an elven archmage whose failed ritual created the shadow entity.
- His past allies, students, or descendants may still influence events.
- His fragmented essence is what the wards contain.

## Campaign Relevance
- Foundational figure for all ward lore and shadow doctrine.
- Key to understanding both restoration and corruption methods.

## Possible First Contact
- Through records, visions, or recovered ritual notes.
