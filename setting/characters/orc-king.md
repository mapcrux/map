# The Orc King

## Group Type
- Sovereign ruler

## Status
- Unmet

## Why They Matter
- Holds territory containing a ward anchor.
- His ceasefire with the dwarves is fragile.
- Could become a powerful ally or a catastrophic enemy.

## Campaign Relevance
- Regional power broker for southern anchor security.
- Diplomacy outcome may determine whether conflict widens.

## Possible First Contact
- Ceasefire council, border parley, or emergency shadow-incursion appeal.
