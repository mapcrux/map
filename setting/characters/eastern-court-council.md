# The Eastern Court Council

## Group Type
- Governing body (elven capital)

## Status
- Unmet

## Why They Matter
- They hold the truth about the ward anchors.
- Deep political divisions exist (Verdant Circle vs. Silver Veil).
- Their reaction to Lethariel's return will shape the campaign.

## Campaign Relevance
- Primary political decision point for alliance legitimacy.
- Can unlock or block access to strategic ward intelligence.

## Possible First Contact
- Formal audience upon arrival at the Eastern Court.
