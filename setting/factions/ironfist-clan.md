# Ironfist Clan

## Type
- Dwarves of Stonebridge

## Status
- Allied

## Motives
- Preserve dwarven honor and craftsmanship
- Protect trade routes and uphold the ceasefire with the Orc King
- Support Thrain's return to arms as a symbol of unity

## Current Role
- Potential reinforcements for Merwing's party.
- Stonebridge forges may produce arms resistant to shadow corruption.

## Notes
- Thrain's return may rally wider dwarven support.
- Clan logistics could become decisive in long-term war readiness.
