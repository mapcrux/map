# Long Rest 1 Milestone Summary

## Arc Title
- **The Whispering Vale and the Sundered One**

## Executive Summary
- Merwing and Lethariel uncovered a coordinated shadow plot to break ancient elven wards in the Whispering Vale.
- A Shadeblade master was defeated, but the ritual damage triggered a deeper threat: the stirring of the Sundered One.
- The pair survived the ruin collapse and set course for the Eastern Court to deliver warning, expose internal sabotage, and seek allied response.

## Milestone Timeline
1. **The Dying Elf's Charge**
	- Merwing receives an ancient map fragment and warning that enemies are hunting ward secrets.
2. **Arrival at Whispering Vale**
	- The party confirms that the Vale's ancient ward network is actively failing.
3. **The Wisp Encounter**
	- A dying ward-echo reveals intrusion, fear, and a path toward compromised ruins.
4. **Rune Investigation**
	- Evidence confirms Shadeblade involvement in ward sabotage.
5. **Battle with Shadewraiths**
	- Merwing and Lethariel discover strong light-and-shadow disruption synergy in tandem combat.
6. **Ritual Discovery**
	- A Shadeblade master attempts to bind or redirect the Sundered One's will.
7. **Ritual Sabotage**
	- Merwing uses the map fragment to destabilize ritual stones and interrupt the binding sequence.
8. **Final Duel and Victory**
	- Coordinated strikes from Merwing and Lethariel defeat the Shadeblade master.
9. **Ruin Collapse and Escape**
	- The Sundered One stirs beneath the Vale; both escape moments before full collapse.
10. **Call to the Eastern Court**
	- Lethariel requests Merwing's continued support for the political and strategic phase ahead.

## Character Milestones

### Merwing
- Accepted stewardship of the map fragment and its burden.
- Demonstrated leadership, tactical calm, and moral resolve under escalating pressure.
- Grew in magical intuition and confidence during ritual disruption.
- Became more emotionally open with Lethariel.

### Lethariel Thalanor
- Transitioned from formal caution to active trust and partnership with Merwing.
- Proved elite ward-lore competence and battlefield command.
- Acknowledged personal vulnerability while maintaining mission focus.
- Began questioning Eastern Court secrecy and possible internal corruption.

### Relationship Milestone: Merwing and Lethariel
- Trust was reinforced through shared risk, synchronized combat, and mutual protection.
- Emotional intimacy increased, though still largely unspoken.
- Both now treat one another as indispensable partners for the next arc.

## Key Locations
- **Whispering Vale:** Ancient elven forest where the ward network restrains the Sundered One.
- **Warded Stones Circle:** Initial sabotage evidence and dying wisp encounter point.
- **Ruins Beneath the Vale:** Ritual site used by the Shadeblade master.
- **Eastern Court (next phase):** Upcoming center for diplomacy, intelligence, and possible betrayal.

## Factions and Named Groups
- **House Thalanor:** Noble line with deep ties to ward-lore stewardship.
- **Eastern Court:** Elven political authority, now suspected of internal compromise.
- **Umbral Covenant:** Exiled elves seeking to harness shadow power rather than seal it.
- **Blackspire Warlocks:** Human cult network pursuing shadow-aligned magical leverage.
- **Shadeblade Masters:** Shadow operatives tied to legendary blades forged in the Deep Hollows.

## Threats, Mysteries, and Intel
- **The Sundered One:** Ancient spirit of shadow and hunger, bound but no longer fully dormant.
- **Shadeblade Theft:** One of three legendary blades was stolen roughly a decade ago and is now active in sabotage operations.
- **Ward Sabotage Pattern:** Attacks indicate insider-level knowledge of elven ward architecture.
- **Possible Court Traitor:** Evidence points to high-access collusion within or near the Eastern Court.
- **Map Fragment Nature:** Artifact appears to be more than cartographic; it resonates with ward magic and can disrupt shadow rituals.

## Current Objective (Post-Long Rest)
- Travel to the Eastern Court with urgency.
- Deliver confirmation of the Shadeblade master's defeat.
- Report the Sundered One's stirring and ward collapse risk.
- Identify traitorous channels aiding shadow-aligned factions.
- Build an elf-human response coalition before the threat escalates.

## Stakes Going Forward
- The Sundered One is stirring.
- The wards are compromised.
- Political trust is uncertain.
- Merwing and Lethariel now stand at the center of the next decisive phase.
