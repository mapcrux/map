# Short Rest 2-1 Milestone Summary

## Context
- **Since Last Long Rest:** Long Rest 2
- **Current Arc:** Eastern Court Approach

## Executive Summary
- The fellowship departed Stonebridge at sunrise and entered the Aelinthar Forest.
- They discovered a failing ward anchor in a hidden grove and successfully restored it using combined magic.
- This confirms the shadow threat is active, coordinated, and accelerating across connected anchor sites.

## Milestone Timeline
1. **Morning Departure from Stonebridge**
   - Merwing led the march, with Lethariel scouting, Thrain on rear guard, Pip ranging ahead, and Elowen offering travel blessings.
   - Morale remained high despite unresolved fears about visions and failing wards.
2. **Entering the Aelinthar Forest**
   - The party encountered an ancient, watchful environment marked by shifting light, buried runes, and magical echoes.
3. **Discovery of the Failing Ward Grove**
   - Merwing traced a rhythmic subterranean "breathing" sound to a hidden grove.
   - Site features included stone sentinels, a sunken pool, and a levitating obsidian shard pulsing with shadow.
   - The group identified the site as a failing ward anchor.
4. **Combined-Magic Restoration**
   - Lethariel and Elowen synchronized moonlit elven magic with Dawnmother-blessed radiance.
   - Their silver-gold lattice stabilized the stones, dissolved the shard, and cleared the pool.
   - The ward anchor was restored and ambient whispering ceased.

## Key Outcomes
- First confirmed restoration of a failing ward anchor during active campaign operations.
- Proof that coordinated ranger, elven, and divine synergy can reverse corruption.
- Significant morale and confidence increase across the fellowship.

## New Knowledge Gained
- The Sundered One's influence is active, not dormant.
- Ward anchors are networked: failure in one weakens others.
- Corruption appears deliberate, not natural decay.
- Prophetic indicators remain accurate: Merwing and Lethariel are central to the convergence.
- Eastern Court secrecy may conceal operationally critical truths.

## Current Objective
- Continue east on the ranger's path toward the Eastern Court.
- Warn the elven council.
- Identify the traitor aiding the Umbral Covenant.

## Current Stakes
- **If successful:**
  - Remaining anchors may be restored.
  - The Sundered One's awakening may be delayed or prevented.
  - Light-shadow balance may be reestablished.
- **If the fellowship fails:**
  - Anchor collapse will cascade.
  - Shadow corruption will spread unchecked.
  - The Sundered One may rise.
