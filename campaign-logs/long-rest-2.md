# Long Rest 2 Milestone Summary

## Arc Title
- **The Recruitment and Fireside Chapter**

## Executive Summary
- Merwing and Lethariel assembled a core fellowship at Stonebridge by recruiting Thrain, Pip, and Elowen.
- Group cohesion strengthened through reunion, shared purpose, and campfire reflection.
- The party entered the Aelinthar Forest and completed the first successful restoration of a corrupted ward anchor.

## Milestone Timeline
1. **Arrival at Stonebridge**
	- Merwing and Lethariel reach the dwarven-built town at dawn.
	- Recruitment phase begins for the eastern campaign.
2. **Thrain's Forge**
	- Merwing reconnects with Thrain Ironfist and convinces him to return from retirement.
	- Party gains frontline strength and veteran tactical support.
3. **Market Encounter**
	- Pip Underbough is caught "checking Merwing's pockets for holes."
	- Pip rejoins the group, restoring stealth and scout capability.
4. **Chapel of the Dawnmother**
	- Elowen greets Merwing and shares new prophetic warnings.
	- Elowen commits to the mission under divine conviction.
5. **Campfire Night**
	- Fellowship bonds through stories, humor, and vulnerability.
	- Emotional trust rises across the party.
6. **Forest Dawn**
	- The group enters Aelinthar Forest.
	- Overland phase begins under increasing magical pressure.
7. **Ward Restoration**
	- Merwing, Lethariel, and Elowen combine ranger, light, and divine techniques.
	- A corrupted ward anchor is restored, weakening local shadow influence.

## Relationship Milestones
- **Merwing and Lethariel:** Trust deepens; shared magic reinforces subtle but growing affection.
- **Merwing and Thrain:** Old camaraderie rekindles with humor and battlefield respect.
- **Merwing and Pip:** Mentor-protege dynamic stabilizes; Pip's loyalty hardens.
- **Merwing and Elowen:** Spiritual alignment grows alongside emotional tension.
- **Lethariel and Elowen:** Mutual admiration develops despite philosophical friction around light vs. divine practice.
- **Thrain and Pip:** Protective bond strengthens; Thrain increasingly treats Pip as family.

## Faction Status Changes
- **Ironfist Clan:** Neutral to Allied
  - Thrain's return may open broader dwarven support channels.
- **Dawnmother's Temple:** Observing to Active
  - Elowen's visions formalize temple engagement.
- **Eastern Court:** Suspicious to Awaiting Contact
  - Lethariel's return is likely to trigger internal political tests.
- **Umbral Covenant:** Unknown to Active
  - Their influence is now suspected in ward corruption operations.

## New Intel and Threats
- **Ward Anchor Corruption:** The Sundered One's influence is propagating through ancient ward-stones.
- **Fragmented Prophecies:** Elowen's visions tie Merwing and Lethariel to a Dawnmother-linked destiny path.
- **Hidden Traitor:** Indicators still point to an Eastern Court collaborator aiding shadow networks.
- **Pip's Pursuers:** Unknown trackers are following Pip, with possible Umbral Covenant ties.

## New Locations Recorded
- **Stonebridge:** Dwarven trade hub and fellowship recruitment point.
- **Eastern Roadway:** Transitional overland route into elven-adjacent wilderness.
- **Aelinthar Forest:** Ancient, semi-sentient woodland containing vulnerable ward infrastructure.
- **Restored Ward Grove:** First confirmed successful ward-anchor recovery site.

## Current Objective (Post-Long Rest)
- Continue east through Aelinthar Forest toward the Eastern Court.
- Warn elven leadership of the Sundered One's awakening trajectory.
- Identify and expose the Court-side traitor.
- Build a united elf-human-dwarf alliance.
- Secure remaining ward anchors before cascading failures begin.

## Stakes Going Forward
- **If successful:** The fellowship can reinforce the ward network, unify factions, and delay or prevent the Sundered One's rise.
- **If they fail:** Ward collapse accelerates, shadow influence spreads, and the setting risks entering a prolonged age of darkness.
